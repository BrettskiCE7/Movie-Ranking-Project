The Best Movies According to Brett

My top 5 movies of all-time

The Dark Knight

Second on the Batman Dark Knight Trilogy and gives a
stunning performance on the chaotic mind of the Joker.

Coco

A pixar animated film based on the importance of family

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

A movie with a unique cell shaded art style based on multipler
versions of our favourite web slinging hero.

Baseketball

A comedy from the minds that gave us South Park, that takes a
combined twist on baseball and basketball.

Beverlly Hills Cop

A late 80s action cop film that oozes charm in my opinion,
when asked about 70s-90s films this comes to mind.
